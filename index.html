About Taimora

Meaning: From Taimako (help) and Aura (hope/light) — Help with Hope.

Taimora Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Northern Nigeria, restoring hope and dignity to vulnerable communities through compassionate aid and sustainable action.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To uplift vulnerable families and children through food assistance, education, healthcare support, and empowerment programs in Northern Nigeria.

Vision: A world where every individual has access to food, opportunity, and a pathway toward a hopeful and self-reliant future.

Food Security

Community feeding & nutrition packs for households in need.

Clean Water & Health

Boreholes, urgent hospital bill support & medical outreach.

Education & Youth

Scholarships, school kits, vocational & digital skills.

Women & Families

Micro-grants and training for livelihood resilience.
