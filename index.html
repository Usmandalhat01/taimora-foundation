About Taimora

Meaning: From Taimako (help) and Aura (hope/light) — Help with Hope.

Taimora Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Nigeria, restoring hope and dignity to vulnerable communities through compassionate aid and sustainable action.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To uplift vulnerable families and children through food assistance, education, healthcare support, and empowerment programs.

Vision: A world where every individual has access to food, opportunity, and a pathway toward a hopeful and self-reliant future.

Our Focus Areas

  • Feeding & Nutrition Relief
  • Clean Water & Health Access
  • Education & Youth Empowerment
  • Women & Family Support
  • Sustainable Community Projects
